The tngovnerds website

We are a group of passionate technologists who work for the State of Tennessee. Our mission is to promote the create of quality software throughout the state with the understand that what we do is done in support of our fellow employees and our fellow citizens.

We are open to anyone who works for the State of Tennessee either as a full-time employee or as a contractor and who is interested in creating software.

Our group is still in its infancy and we need your help to grow. Interested in joining us? Send us an email.
