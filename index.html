who we are

We are a group of passionate technologists who work for the State of Tennessee. Our mission is to promote the development of quality software throughout the state, with the understanding that what we do is in support of our fellow employees and our fellow citizens.

We are open to anyone who works for the State of Tennessee - either as a full-time employee or as a contractor - and is interested in creating high-quality software.

Our group is still in its infancy and we need your help to grow. Interested in joining us? Send us an email.

what's happening

We are preparing for our very first meeting! Plans are currently underway for the inaugural meeting of the tngovnerds. Mark your calendar for Monday, Jan 27, 2014. We'll announce the location and time shortly, but expect it to be somewhere near downtown Nashville and sometime late afternoon / early evening. Check back or shoot us an email to find out more.

comming soon

We're just getting started, so we haven't quite got everything sorted yet. Don't worry, we'll get there.
